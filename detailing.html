Premium Car Care at In The Basement Car Valet Centre

At In The Basement Car Valet Centre, we treat every vehicle with meticulous attention to detail — delivering a premium car care experience in a private, secure basement location.

Our offerings span from expertly executed basic washes to comprehensive full valets and bespoke detailing packages. Additional detailing services include undercarriage and engine bay jet washing, black trim treatment, headlight polishing, full exterior polishing, deep cleaning of seats, roof lining and carpets, micro valets, mini valets, and full valets, ceramic coatings, exterior polishing, and full-spectrum of detailing.

Price Range:

  • Standard Washes: R180 – R300
  • Micro Valets: R280 – R500
  • Mini Valets: R850 – R2 500
  • Full Valets & Detailing: R2 000 – R5 000
