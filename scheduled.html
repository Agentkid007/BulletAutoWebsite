DID YOU KNOW?

Your vehicle should be serviced every 10 000km for Diesel vehicles and every 15 000km for Petrol vehicles.

Suggested Maintenance Schedule:

  • Brakes should be replaced every 75 000km
  • Cam Belt should be replaced every 90 000km
  • Shocks should be replaced every 80 000km
  • Brake Fluid should be replaced every 40 000km
  • Cooling system should be flushed every 100 000km
  • Belt & Hose check every 70 000km
  • Tyre Rotation every 10 000km
